Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

FloatSwim ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website floatswim.org. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our site.

Information We Collect

Information you provide

When you submit a form on our site (such as the Share a Story form or the Get Involved form on our Roadmap page), we collect the information you enter, which may include your name, email address, and message content. These forms are processed through Formspree, a third-party form handling service.

Information collected automatically

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors use our site. GA4 may collect:

  • Pages visited, time spent on pages, and navigation paths
  • Device type, browser, operating system, and screen resolution
  • General geographic location (city/region level, not precise location)
  • Referring website or search engine

Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

How We Use Your Information

  • Form submissions: To read and respond to your stories, questions, or collaboration requests. We will never publish a story without your explicit written consent.
  • Analytics data: To understand which pages are most helpful, improve site content, and measure the reach of our water safety resources.
  • Site improvement: To fix technical issues, optimize performance, and prioritize new content.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

FloatSwim uses the following third-party services:

  • GitHub Pages: Hosts our website. See GitHub's Privacy Statement.
  • Google Analytics 4: Provides anonymous website usage statistics. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Formspree: Processes form submissions. See Formspree's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts: Serves web fonts. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Amazon Associates: Some product links on our site are affiliate links. When you click these links and make a purchase, Amazon may collect information according to their Privacy Notice. We earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.

Cookies

Our site uses cookies primarily through Google Analytics. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us understand how you use our site. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies will not prevent you from using FloatSwim, but some analytics features may not function.

Children's Privacy

FloatSwim is designed for parents, caregivers, and water safety professionals. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted personal information through our forms, please contact us so we can remove it.

Data Retention

Form submissions are retained in our Formspree account for as long as needed to respond and fulfill the purpose of the submission. Google Analytics data is retained according to Google's standard retention policies. You may request deletion of your submitted information at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to any personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent for story publication at any time
  • Opt out of Google Analytics tracking

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise your rights, you can reach us through the Share a Story form or the Get Involved form on our Roadmap page.
